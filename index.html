My Hobbies

I have two important hobbies that I hold dear to my heart: Music and Video Games.

Music

I feel that music is an integral part of my life, and my life would be very boring without it. In middle school and high school,
I was part of the band and I played percussion (snare drum, bass drum, marimba, etc.) I've grown to love all types of genres, ranging
from classical to progressive metal. The genres I am currently listening to are R&B, Electronic, Prog Metal, and Rock. My favorite band
right now is called Polyphia, which is a prog metal group. I've learned how to play the piano, drum set, and four-mallet instruments.
Currently, I'm coaching high school kids for percussion in Lake Elsinore!

Video Games

I've played video games since I was little, and it's the hobby that I've most wasted my time on. My favorite game genres are FPSes (first-person
shooters), MOBAs (multiplayer online battle arena), open-world, RPGs (role-playing games), sandbox games, and rhythm games. I mostly played on
Xbox when I was a kid--playing FPSes like Call of Duty and Halo-- and I slowly transitioned to PC gaming. On PC, I mainly play Overwatch and League of
Legends, but I like playing sandbox games like Factorio and Terraria. My favorite game right now is the Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild on Nintendo Switch.
